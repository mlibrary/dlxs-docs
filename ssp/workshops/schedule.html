SSP Text Encoding and User Support Workshop


Dates: July 22-25, 1998
Instructors: Maria Bonn and Chris Powell, University of Michigan, and Perry Willett, Indiana University
The instructors will be supported by Nigel Kerr and Alan Pagliere.

Course objectives

  1. Become familiar with the basics of text encoding using SGML
  2. Introduce principles of document analysis, including DTD reading and using PAT
  3. Discuss issues of interface design and build a rudimentary interface to a sample collection
  4. Gain a basic understanding of the process of deploying an electronic collection for Web access
  5. Discuss issues of user support for electronic collections and of the relationship of the digital library to the traditional library

Required readings:

  • SGML Primer: Introduction and Body
  • The Gentle Introduction to SGML from the TEI Guidelines
  • Introductory portions of the Pat 5.0 manual
