Function instead of Appearance


<POEM>
<TITLE>Lines Written in Early Spring</TITLE>
<AUTHOR>William Wordsworth</AUTHOR>
<STANZA>
<LINE>I heard a thousand blended notes,</LINE>
<LINE>While in grove I sate reclined,</LINE>
<LINE>In that sweet mood when pleasant thoughts</LINE>
<LINE>Bring sad thoughts to the mind.</LINE>
</STANZA>
<STANZA>
<LINE>To her fair works did nature link</LINE>
<LINE>The human soul that through me ran;</LINE>
<LINE>And much it griev'd me my heart to think</LINE>
<LINE>What man has made of man.</LINE>
</STANZA>
</POEM>
