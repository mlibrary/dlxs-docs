What is SGML? (10)

What’s the difference between SGML and XML?

  • Has related standards for linking and styles

  • Has some differences in tag syntax

  • Lacks some functionality that simplifies DTD writing

  • XML documents must declare that they are XML

    • <?xml version="1.0" ?>

  • Documents don’t have to be delivered with their DTD

    • Just "well-formed" according to the standard

  • W3C comparison: http://www.w3.org/TR/NOTE-sgml-xml-971215
