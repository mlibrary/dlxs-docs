Text components in TEI Lite

  • What are divisions composed of?

    • prose is mostly paragraphs (<p>)
    • verse is mostly lines (<l>), sometimes in hierarchic groups (<lg>)
    • drama is mostly speeches (<sp>) containing <p> or <l> and interspersed with stage directions (<stage>) and speakers (<speaker>)

  • These may be mixed within divisions, and may also appear directly within undivided texts.
