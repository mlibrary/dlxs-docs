Course Objectives

Become familiar with the DLXS Middleware. This means basically understanding the following:

  • XPat and XPat searching
  • Document Classes
  • Middleware
    • Basic architecture of the systems involved

    • How to modify the middleware

We won't cover:

  • Data preparation and XPat indexing of data (covered in the Encoding Workshop)
  • Details of installation, though we will cover the work we are doing on making installation more straightforward
