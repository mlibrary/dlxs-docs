Course objectives

  • Become familiar with the DLXS middleware (including searching with XPat, document classes, and how to modify the middleware). Objectives details.

Prerequisites

  • some familiarity with SGML
  • familiarity with perl5 (do the exercise)
  • have seen the kinds of online collections DLPS makes available already (see http://www.umdl.umich.edu/ and http://www.hti.umich.edu/ for examples)
  • have the XPAT package from DLXS
  • Note: during the workshop, we will provide data, already indexed and ready for use. Information on data preparation can be found at: DLXS Text Encoders Workshop
