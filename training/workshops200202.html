DLXS Workshop, Winter 2002

The Collection Implementor's and Programmer's workshops have been combined in to a single workshop for 2002. The focus will be on Text Class and Image Class and will emphasize installation, configuration, and content preparation.

Workshop Details:

  • Preliminary Schedule:Basic schedule of the 5 and 1/2 day workshop
  • Outline: Image Class Collection Implementation (Thursday and Friday of workshop)
  • Outline: Text Class Collection Implementation (Tuesday afternoon and Wednesday of workshop)
  • Materials for other portions of workshop forthcoming.

Instructors: Phil Farber, Alan Pagliere, Chris Powell, Matt Stoeffler, John Weise, University of Michigan

Start: Monday, February 25, 2002
Finish: Saturday, March 2, 2002 -- ending at noon

Each morning runs from 9 am to 12 pm with a break.
Each afternoon session from 1:30 pm to 4:30 pm with a break.
The last day, Saturday, will end at noon.

You will be on your own for lunch and dinner.

Location: Faculty Exploratory, second floor Hatcher Graduate Library, University of Michigan (central campus map)
